How It Works

  • Submit Local Issue

    Describe the maintenance or development issue in your ward. Optionally attach a photo or record a short voice summary.

  • Community Upvotes

    Other citizens browse public submissions and upvote. Urgent local issues naturally rise to the top.

  • Office Prioritization

    The MP reviews submissions filtered by theme/area and updates their status from 'Received' to 'Prioritized'.

Tips for Effective Submission

  • Be Specific

    Instead of saying "Water issue", specify "Drinking water pipe broken near Gate No. 2 of the primary school".

  • Verify Location

    Include landmark references so field officers sent by the MP can locate the site easily.

  • Add Photos

    Visuals speed up reviews. Submissions with photos get processed 30% faster.
